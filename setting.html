FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I calculate my GWA?
For college: Enter your subject names, units, and grades (1.0 to 4.0), then click "CALCULATE GWA". For high school: Enter subject names and grades (0-100), then click "CALCULATE".
What's the difference between High School and College mode?
High School mode calculates simple average of grades (0-100). College mode calculates weighted average (GWA) based on units/credits with grades from 1.0 (highest) to 4.0 (lowest).
Can I save my calculations?
Yes! In College mode, after calculating your GWA, a "DOWNLOAD PDF" button appears. Click it to generate and save a beautifully formatted PDF report of your grades.
How do I add more subjects?
Click the "ADD ANOTHER SUBJECT" button to add more subject rows. You can add as many as you need. To remove a subject, click the red X button next to it.
Can I change the theme?
Yes! Open the menu and select your preferred theme: Pink Mode or Blue Mode. Your choice will be saved automatically.
Is my data saved locally?
Yes, your theme preference is saved in your browser's local storage. However, your grade calculations are not automatically saved - you should use the PDF download feature to save your results.
